EAST to WEST

Artist shows a birds eye view into some of your favorite TV show's apartments and homes.

Iñaki Aliste Lizarralde is not only a young professional interior designer but as a hobby hand draws incredible floor plans of popular TV series and movies. These floor plans show you a different perspective of apartments and homes you've vicariously lived from episode to episode.

The collection includes the likes of The Simpson, Dexter, Friends, Seinfeld, Two and a Half Men, The Big Bang Theory, How I Met Your Mother, and a handful others. Each of the floor plans is highly detailed, and not only includes the architectural side of things, but also goes into immense furnishing details really bringing these television shows to life. As a huge fan of a lot of these shows, this was definitely a project we could appreciate the amount of time and effort that was put in. Check out the floor plans below.

The Floor Plans

Seinfeld

This is the apartment of Jerry Seinfeld from his show Seinfeld. Sets have changed throughout the different seasons and this floorplan is a mixture of all of them.This is a hand drawed plan scaleled and coloured pencil rendering with full detail down to the furniture and fabric.

The Simpsons

These are the floorplans of the Simpson Family house from the TV series The Simpsons. This is an original hand drawed plan, scaled to size and coloured with colour pens and with full details of furniture and cartoon design.

Sex and the City

This is apartment at 64 Perry St in Manhattan’s West Village, will likely forever be known as Carrie Bradshaw’s home in Sex and the City. Carrie Bradshaw, New York’s most fabulous single lady, lived in a $700 a month rent controlled apartment on the Upper East Side, and this is the floor plan. Carrie’s iconic brownstone was the setting for many scenes on the show and where she sat looking out the window while writing her column.
